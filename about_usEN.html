Abous Us

Ecomics Media, a leading company in the global digital comics market, has led the comics market by developing ECOMICS, Korea's first Internet comics service, in 1998. With years of experience in entering overseas markets and translating and editing know-hows, it currently has the largest number of overseas digital comics distribution channels among domestic companies. Since 2006, it has been providing various Korean and Japanese comics to English-speaking readers through its U.S. subsidiary, NETCOMICS (NETCOMICS, Inc., netcomics.com), via the website, apps, and books. Since 2014, it has expanded its export scope to Japan, China, Indonesia, Thailand, Taiwan, Vietnam, Spain, France, Germany, etc. and is now set to enter more global markets.
